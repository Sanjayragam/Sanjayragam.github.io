Science, Technology, Engineering and Math Education
STEM-Ed (Science, Technology, Engineering and Math Education )is a four-month-long mentoring cum project presentation program focused on school students and teachers intending to help children develop their skill sets in STEM. It focus on improving the attitude of students toward STEM fields and careers. It is packed with vibrant and insightful training hands-on session which increase the self-confidence of the students in tackling science classes and projects at the very basic level itself.
